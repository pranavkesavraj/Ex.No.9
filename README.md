# Aim:
To demonstrate the ability of text-to-video generation tools to create a cyberpunk music video by crafting precise prompts, synchronizing city visuals and dancer movements with an electronic beat.

# Question:

How can AI create a cyberpunk music video where neon-lit streets and dancers move in sync with an electronic beat?
# Procedure:
### 1.Analyze the Original/Reference Video:

Identify objects/subjects: dancers, neon signs, futuristic vehicles, cityscape.

Observe colors: neon pink, purple, blue, electric green.

Note textures: glossy wet streets, metallic surfaces, reflections.

Examine lighting: neon glow, shadows, reflections.

Record composition & style: wide city shots, close-ups, aerial perspectives, cinematic cyberpunk aesthetic.

(Insert a screenshot of the original video with labeled elements.)

### 2.Create Basic Prompt:

Start with a simple description:

“A cyberpunk city at night with neon-lit streets and dancers moving in sync with electronic music.”

(Include a screenshot of the basic prompt.)

### 3.Refine Prompt with More Details:

Add colors, textures, choreography, and mood:

“A futuristic cyberpunk megacity at night, neon-lit streets glowing in pink, purple, and blue. Dancers in edgy outfits move dynamically in sync with an electronic beat. Wet streets reflect neon lights, holographic signs flicker, and futuristic vehicles pass by. Cinematic wide shots and close-ups capture the vibrant, edgy, and rhythmic nightlife.”

(Include diagram or mood board images.)

### 4.Specify Style and Artistic Influence:

Add cinematic or artistic style:

“In the style of a high-energy cyberpunk movie, with glossy textures, glowing reflections, and dynamic motion. Neon colors dominate, with a futuristic, edgy, and rhythmic atmosphere.”

### 5.Adjust and Fine-Tune:

Incorporate motion sync, camera dynamics, and visual effects (rain reflections, holograms).

Iterate prompt based on AI output until desired result is achieved.

(Include screenshots of generated video frames vs. original frames.)

### 6.Generate Video:

Use text-to-video AI tools (DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, MidJourney) with refined prompt.

### 7.Compare Generated Video with Original:

Assess colors, lighting, composition, subjects, style.

Refine prompts and document changes.

### 8.Save and Document:

Save all generated videos.

Record basic → refined → final prompts with observations.
